INTGR8 Technologies Last Mile Delivery Vehicle

Background

This past summer (2020) I worked as an engineering intern for INTGR8 Technologies. One of the things I did for them was design concepts and renderings for a semi-autonomous Last Mile delivery vehicle using Fusion 360. This helped me significantly improve my CAD, design, and creative thinking skills.

The Project

A couple different models were created however, I will show only one of them. Without talking about the entire concept of the vehicle, I’ll describe it. This vehicle is meant to be semi-autonomous, thus doesn’t have a driver area. Since it is autonomous, there are some small windows around the top end of the car where cameras are to be placed. This concept features side and front doors as well as a raising roof and floor. Moreover, there are tracks on the floor for the shelving units to move on, which feature a variety of different size containers based on PO box sizes.

I created everything in this design, no parts were taking from external resources. Please look at the annotated slideshow below to view the renderings.
